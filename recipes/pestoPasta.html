Pesto pasta

There is nothing like a good plate of pesto pasta after a long day of coding. By having for dinner pesto pasta you will have the best humanly possible sleep and be ready to keep grinding the next day!

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ tablespoons pesto
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 (16 ounce) package pasta
  • salt to taste
  • ground black pepper to taste
